Voice & Tone

Location Central Point, Oregon — Southern Oregon's Rogue Valley
Contact hello@ponderosa.coffee
Sourcing Beans sourced from Mythical Coffee, Gilbert, AZ — a trusted small roaster relationship
Values Coffee · Community · The outdoors · Nature · Peace
Warm Write like a neighbor who loves coffee. Familiar without being familiar.
Unhurried Short sentences, gentle pacing. Never push, never rush. Let the reader linger.
Grounded Rooted in place — southern Oregon, the pines, the land. Honest over clever.
Quiet Confidence without noise. No exclamation marks, no hype. Let the quality speak.
Inviting Open and approachable. Every touchpoint should feel like an open door.
Personal Owner-operated, small-batch. Use "we" carefully — often "I" or no pronoun is more honest.
We are → not
Warm → Sentimental
Simple → Sparse or cold
Confident → Boastful
Grounded → Precious or pretentious
Inviting → Salesy or urgent
Thoughtful → Overthought
Writing Principles
01 Write at the pace of a pour Short sentences. White space. Let the reader breathe. If a sentence can be cut without losing meaning, cut it.
02 Name the place, name the thing Be specific. "Ethiopian natural process" beats "great beans." Specificity earns trust and signals care.
03 No hype words Avoid: amazing, perfect, best, love, obsessed, incredible, excited. These words cost credibility and add noise.
04 Lowercase where possible The brand name and logo live in lowercase. Follow that lead in copy — it feels softer and more approachable.
05 Let silence work Not every post needs a call to action. Sometimes the right word is no word at all — a photo, a dot, a pause.
06 Earn the emotion Don't tell people how to feel. Describe what's real — the smell, the morning light, the process — and trust the reader.
Copy Examples
Do

a slow morning. a good cup. that's the whole idea.

small batch. central point, oregon. sourced with care.

washed ethiopian. bright, clean, a little floral.

come find us at the market saturday.

we love coffee, community, and a good walk outside.

Don't

We are SO excited to announce our AMAZING new blend!!! ☕🎉

The best coffee you'll ever taste — guaranteed!

LIMITED TIME OFFER — get it before it's gone!

Our passion for coffee is what sets us apart from the rest.
